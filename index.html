Information technology (IT) is the broad use of computers, software, networks, and other hardware to create, store, process, retrieve, transmit, and manipulate data and information, particularly within a business or organizational context. Key aspects include managing data, enabling communication, supporting business operations, and driving innovation through tools like cloud computing, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence (AI), and data analytics. IT is crucial for modern businesses, allowing them to improve efficiency, decision-making, customer support, and market access, and has become a vital part of daily life.
